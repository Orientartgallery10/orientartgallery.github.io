Who We Are.

Guardians of
Sacred Script

Orient Art Gallery was founded from a lifelong connection to the world of Islamic art, craftsmanship, and framing. What began as a small family business in Johannesburg has grown over generations into one of South Africa’s most recognised names in Islamic calligraphy and bespoke framing, built on a deep respect for the beauty, meaning, and heritage of sacred Arabic script. Our work brings together traditional calligraphy, custom framing, and refined interior presentation to create pieces that feel timeless within both classical and contemporary spaces. From the flowing elegance of Thuluth and Diwani to the bold geometry of Kufic, every artwork is approached with care, balance, and attention to detail. We work closely with skilled calligraphers and artisans to produce handcrafted pieces for homes, masjids, offices, and architectural interiors across the country. Whether creating a single statement artwork or designing a complete installation, our focus remains the same: meaningful craftsmanship, lasting quality, and work that carries both visual presence and spiritual depth. Alongside our artworks, we offer bespoke framing, custom sizing, consultation services, and nationwide delivery, helping clients bring the richness of Islamic calligraphy into spaces designed to be lived in and remembered.

Expert Consultation

We Guide You
From Concept to Wall

Not sure what to order, where to place it, or what size would suit your space best? Our consultation service is here to guide you through every detail with genuine expertise, thoughtful advice, and a deep appreciation for this art form.

Interior Consultation

We assess your interior — its style, lighting, wall dimensions, and existing decor — to recommend calligraphy that elevates the room without competing with it.

  • Residential homes & apartments
  • Office & boardroom spaces
  • Masjid & Islamic centre design
  • Hotel lobbies & commercial spaces

Artwork Placement

The right artwork in the wrong position loses its power. We advise on hanging height, focal point alignment, wall positioning, and lighting for maximum visual impact.

  • Eye-level & focal point analysis
  • Multi-piece wall arrangement
  • Lighting & shadow considerations
  • Entrance & reception positioning

Custom Sizing

Every wall is unique. We determine the perfect dimensions for your space — whether an intimate A3 desk piece or a monumental feature wall installation spanning metres.

  • Wall dimension measurement
  • Visual balance & proportion
  • Intimate to monumental scale
  • Series & set compositions

Space & Theme Layout

Creating a cohesive Islamic aesthetic across an entire space? We curate scripts, styles, and arrangements that work together — telling a visual story throughout your interior.
