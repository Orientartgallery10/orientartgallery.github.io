Who We Are.

Guardians of
Sacred Script

Orient Art Gallery was born from a deep reverence for the Arabic language and the living art of Islamic calligraphy. We are a studio of dedicated calligraphers — each trained in the classical traditions of Thuluth, Naskh, Diwani, and Kufic — bringing centuries-old craft into the modern world with full integrity and care.

Every piece is created entirely by hand. No digital shortcuts. No printed reproductions. Each stroke is deliberate, each word rendered with the full weight of its meaning. We believe calligraphy is not mere decoration — it is devotion made visible on paper, canvas, and wood.

Beyond individual artworks, we provide complete custom framing, nationwide shipping, and personal consultation services for homes, masjids, offices, businesses, and any interior that deserves the quiet power of sacred script on its walls.

Expert Consultation

We Guide You
From Concept to Wall

Not sure what to order, where to place it, or how large it should be? Our consultation service answers every question — with warmth, genuine expertise, and a deep love for this art form.

Interior Consultation

We assess your interior — its style, lighting, wall dimensions, and existing decor — to recommend calligraphy that elevates the room without competing with it.

  • Residential homes & apartments
  • Office & boardroom spaces
  • Masjid & Islamic centre design
  • Hotel lobbies & commercial spaces

Artwork Placement

The right artwork in the wrong position loses its power. We advise on hanging height, focal point alignment, wall positioning, and lighting for maximum visual impact.

  • Eye-level & focal point analysis
  • Multi-piece wall arrangement
  • Lighting & shadow considerations
  • Entrance & reception positioning

Custom Sizing

Every wall is unique. We determine the perfect dimensions for your space — whether an intimate A3 desk piece or a monumental feature wall installation spanning metres.

  • Wall dimension measurement
  • Visual balance & proportion
  • Intimate to monumental scale
  • Series & set compositions

Space & Theme Layout

Creating a cohesive Islamic aesthetic across an entire space? We curate scripts, styles, and arrangements that work together — telling a visual story throughout your interior.
